Account & Privacy

Delete your account

You can request deletion of your Philosophize account and all associated data at any time. Here's how.

1
Send a deletion request

Email us at philosophizelearn@gmail.com with the subject line "Delete My Account". Include the email address associated with your account so we can locate it.

2
We confirm your request

We'll reply within 3 business days to confirm we've received your request and verify your identity.

3
Your account is deleted

Once confirmed, your account and all personal data will be permanently deleted within 30 days.
